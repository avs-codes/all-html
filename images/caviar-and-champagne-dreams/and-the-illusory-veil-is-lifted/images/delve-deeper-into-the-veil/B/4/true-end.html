Whereas before you were content with your newfound state of being.

It was a selfish victory, as you left everyone else still imprisoned by the veil.

Your blissful ignornance made your triumph hollow and cruel.

However, you may now live content knowing that others will no longer be trapped behind the veil.

Now rest, for you may soon be needed again.
